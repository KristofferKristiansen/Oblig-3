Return and delivery

Delivery

- Every order will be shipped within 24 hours
- Normal delivery time 3-4 days
- Trace your order here: trackmyparcel.com

Do you wish to return a product?

- Wrap the package in the original wrapping paper
- Close the bag and attach the returnlabel
- Deliver the package to your closest delivery point
- When the package is returned, you will be refunded

Refund

- If you want to exchange items, then you can place a new orderafter sending the package back
- After the returned product is checked, you will be refunded
